The cuinary art of the food truck vendor has been steadily improving over the last 10 years with fine offerings from Antonio's Italian, Organic on the Road, and now Curbside Thai. Sajja Adulet is to be congratulations for offering delicious and authentic Thai. and authentic Thai food at a reasonale price. We think Curbside Thai provides the finest Asian food on or off the streets of Charlotte.

Steaming rice with a spicy Asian sauce is common street fare and we all accept its limitations in exchange for the convenience of a quick bite on the run. WIth Curbside Thai you don't have compromise. Building on his experience as a master chef at serveral fine establishments on the East Coast, Sajja Adulet has brought the taste of fine dining to the Food Cart Wars and we couldn't be happier.
